# Chennai Logistics Hub

## Hub Thesis

Chennai should be segmented into operating submarkets rather than treated as one metro logistics hub. The Apr 26 warehouse research shows separate demand patterns across Sriperumbudur-Oragadam industrial freight, Redhills/Thiruvallur distribution warehousing, Poonamallee-Irungattukottai gateway movement, Koyambedu-Madhavaram transporter density, and Guindy-Siruseri south-Chennai support lanes.

The immediate opportunity is not only enterprise warehousing. It is the gap between growing warehouse capacity and fragmented transport execution: vehicle discovery, dispatch reliability, rate benchmarking, dock timing, payment visibility, and return-load planning.

## Cluster Map

| Cluster | Role | Approx. Captured Sq Ft | Key Demand | Zippy Angle |
|---------|------|------------------------|------------|-------------|

| Koyambedu-Madhavaram | Urban transport nerve center | 0.8 Mn | Local transporters, brokered freight, urban warehouse movement | Supply aggregation, urgent truck discovery, rate visibility |
| Guindy-Siruseri-Okkiyam | South Chennai support zone | 0.6 Mn | OMR/GST Road, IT/industrial support, 3PL/e-commerce | Consumption-zone service and city-industrial support lanes |

## Distinctions That Matter

| Zone | Primary Buyer / Operator | Freight Behavior | Avoid This Mistake |
|------|--------------------------|------------------|--------------------|
| Chennai city / Koyambedu-Madhavaram | Transport brokers, local carriers, urban warehouses | Fast matching, fragmented supply, shorter cycles | Treating it like a Grade A park sales motion |
| Sriperumbudur-Oragadam | Manufacturers, industrial parks, 3PLs | Recurring B2B lanes, plant timing, higher SLA expectations | Selling only spot booking instead of lane reliability |
| Redhills-Thiruvallur | Warehouse operators, e-commerce/FMCG/retail distributors | Repeat outbound dispatch, short-haul, middle-mile | Ignoring dock windows and daily vehicle-pool planning |
| Poonamallee-Irungattukottai | Gateway operators, private godowns, local 3PLs | Transitional movement between city freight and industrial demand | Missing its bridging role between Redhills, Chennai, and Sriperumbudur |

## Warehouse Economics

Apr 26 pricing records suggest Chennai warehouse rents vary materially by grade:

| Grade / Listing Type | Directional Rent Signal |
|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Grade A+ | Around Rs.63/sqft/month |
| Grade A | Around Rs.25/sqft/month |
| Standard / private warehouse or godown | Around Rs.13-16/sqft/month |

Premium facilities are more likely to value visibility, SLAs, integration, and analytics. Standard and private godowns may be more sensitive to fast matching, phone-first workflows, flexible vehicle supply, and transparent rates.
