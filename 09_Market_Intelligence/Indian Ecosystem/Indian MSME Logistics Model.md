# Indian MSME Logistics Model

## Overview

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in India face unique logistics challenges requiring tailored solutions.

## MSME Landscape in India

| Category | Employees | Annual Turnover |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Micro | 1-9 | Up to ₹5 crore |
| Small | 10-49 | ₹5-50 crore |
| Medium | 50-249 | ₹50-250 crore |

### MSME Distribution by Sector
- **Manufacturing**: 30%
- **Trade/Retail**: 35%
- **Services**: 25%
- **Agriculture**: 10%

## Logistics Pain Points

### Cost Challenges
- Small order quantities
- Irregular shipping patterns
- Limited bargaining power
- Multiple supplier coordination

### Operational Challenges
- Limited warehouse space
- Manual documentation
- E-way bill compliance complexity
- Payment collection delays

### Service Challenges
- Reliability concerns
- Tracking limitations
- Communication gaps
- Limited carrier options

## Tailored Solutions

| MSME Need | Platform Solution |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Small shipments | Shared truck load options |
| Irregular patterns | Flexible scheduling |
| Documentation | Auto E-way bill generation |
| Payments | Credit facility + digital payments |
| Reliability | SLA-backed service |

## Pricing Model for MSMEs

| Order Size | Pricing Approach |
|------------|------------------|
| <500 kg | Per-shipment flat rate |
| 500-2000 kg | Per-kg rate |
| >2000 kg | Truck load rate |
| Regular volume | Subscription discount |
