## Revenue Streams

1. **Platform Commission**: From driver earnings (10%)
2. **Service Fees**: From transport companies (₹700/order)
3. **Delivery Charges**: Distance-based pricing
4. **Urgency Premiums**: Time-critical deliveries
5. **Value-Added Services**: Insurance, tracking, etc.
6. **Platform Fees**: Segment-based fee layer applied on top of transport services
7. **Backhaul Discounts**: Commercial incentive to reduce deadhead on favorable return corridors

## Expansion Wedge

- Tier-2 and Tier-3 South India is attractive where MSME density, warehouse growth, and deadhead inefficiency overlap.
- Business-model strength in these markets often comes from better reliability and empty-mile reduction rather than pure commission extraction.
- Embedded finance and faster settlement can be part of the moat when local supply is fragmented.
