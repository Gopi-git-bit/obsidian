# Scenario - Excess Capacity from Partner

## Situation

Partner transport company has excess vehicle capacity available. They are seeking additional loads to improve their fleet utilization.

## Variables

| Variable | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Vehicle type | Various | Capacity available |
| Location | Partner base | Routing to customers |
| Duration | One-time/Ongoing | Depends on context |
| Pricing | Negotiable | Market rate |

## Decision Questions

1. **Current demand?** Need for additional capacity?
2. **Vehicle compatibility?** Matches customer needs?
3. **Pricing terms?** Competitive rate?
4. **Quality assurance?** Partner reliability?

## Recommended Response

```
1. RECEIVE capacity availability from partner
2. ASSESS current demand and near-term needs
3. MATCH available vehicles with potential orders
4. NEGOTIATE terms (price, timeline, terms)
5. ASSIGN orders if terms are acceptable
6. MONITOR partner performance on assigned orders
```

## Benefits

- Improved fleet utilization for partner
- Additional capacity for peak demand
- Relationship building with partners
- Revenue opportunity
