# Scenario - Partner Network Utilization

## Situation

Platform has excess demand and needs to utilize partner transporter network to fulfill orders beyond own fleet capacity.

## Variables

| Variable | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Demand level | High | Exceeds own fleet |
| Partner availability | Available | Network ready |
| Pricing | Market rate | Commission applies |
| Quality | Verified | Partner vetted |

## Recommended Response

```
1. ASSESS current own fleet capacity vs demand
2. IDENTIFY available partner transporters
3. MATCH partner vehicles to order requirements
4. NEGOTIATE terms with partners
5. ASSIGN orders with clear expectations
6. MONITOR partner performance closely
7. MAINTAIN quality standards
```
