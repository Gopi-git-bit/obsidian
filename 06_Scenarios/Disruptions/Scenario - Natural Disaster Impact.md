# Scenario - Natural Disaster Impact

## Situation

Floods, cyclones, earthquakes, or other natural disasters affect logistics operations in a region. Active shipments are at risk.

## Variables

| Variable | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Disaster type | Varies | Regional |
| Affected area | Geographic | Routes impacted |
| Duration | Unknown | Depends on event |
| Safety | Priority | Lives matter most |

## Decision Questions

1. **Safety first?** Driver and cargo safety
2. **Active shipments?** Which orders are at risk?
3. **Rerouting?** Alternative routes available?
4. **Customer communication?** Proactive updates?

## Recommended Response

```
1. ASSESS disaster impact on operations
2. IDENTIFY affected active orders
3. COMMUNICATE with drivers in affected area
4. HOLD shipments in safe locations
5. COORDINATE with emergency services if needed
6. NOTIFY all affected customers
7. PLAN recovery operations post-disaster
```

## Risks

- Driver safety → Priority concern
- Cargo damage → Insurance claims
- Extended delays → Customer dissatisfaction
