# Scenario - Customer Escalation

## Situation

Customer escalates issue to senior management - multiple complaints, threat of legal action, or social media exposure risk.

## Variables

| Variable | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Escalation reason | Various | Delay/Quality/Service |
| Customer status | Premium | VIP or major account? |
| Social risk | High | Media exposure |
| Legal risk | Possible | Lawyer involvement |

## Recommended Response

```
1. RECEIVE escalation immediately
2. ASSIGN dedicated senior handler
3. CONTACT customer within 2 hours
4. LISTEN and acknowledge concerns fully
5. PROPOSE resolution with timeline
6. ESCALATE internally for resources
7. KEEP leadership informed
8. FOLLOW UP proactively
9. DOCUMENT learnings for improvement
```
