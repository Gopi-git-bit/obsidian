# MSME Shipper Pain Points

## Definition

Common challenges faced by Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises when managing logistics for their business.

## Core Pain Points

### 1. Cost Concerns

| Pain Point | Impact | Opportunity |
|------------|--------|-------------|
| High logistics cost | Erodes margins | Competitive pricing |
| Unclear pricing | Budget uncertainty | [[Dynamic Pricing Logic]] |
| Hidden charges | Trust issues | Transparent billing |
| Payment terms | Cash flow stress | Flexible payments |

### 2. Reliability Issues

- **Delayed deliveries** - Production disruptions
- **Damage claims** - Financial losses
- **No visibility** - Customer dissatisfaction
- **Missed SLAs** - Reputation damage

### 3. Process Complexity

- **Documentation burden** - E-way bills, invoices
- **Multiple transporter coordination** - Chaos
- **Limited access** - No digital tools
- **Manual follow-ups** - Time wasted

### 4. Service Gaps

- **Limited vehicle types** - Can't meet needs
- **No single solution** - Fragmented providers
- **Inconsistent quality** - Unpredictable
- **Poor communication** - No updates
