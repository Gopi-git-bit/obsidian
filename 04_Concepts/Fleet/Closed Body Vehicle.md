# Closed Body Vehicle

## Definition

Vehicles with an enclosed cargo compartment (box body) that protects goods from weather, dust, and external visibility.

## Types

| Type | Capacity | Use Case |
|------|----------|----------|
| Closed Body LCV | 500-1500 kg | Urban delivery, small packages |
| Closed Body MCV | 2-7 tons | Regional distribution |
| Closed Body HCV | 10-20 tons | Long-haul, high-value cargo |

## When Required

- **Electronics** - Protection from dust and moisture
- **Pharma** - Temperature-sensitive products
- **Textiles** - Protection from rain/damage
- **Food items** - Hygiene requirements
- **High-value goods** - Security from theft

## Key Variables

- Vehicle dimensions (L×W×H)
- Payload capacity
- Loading method (side/rear)
- Temperature control option

- Higher per-km cost than open body
- Better cargo protection justifies premium
- Limited for return loads
