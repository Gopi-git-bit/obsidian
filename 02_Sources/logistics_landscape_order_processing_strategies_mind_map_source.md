## Extracted Map

### Order Processing Strategies

- make-to-stock (MTS): standard products, forecast-driven, high utilization, short delivery times
- make-to-order (MTO): customer order-driven, individualized products, lower stock costs, variable lot sizes
- assemble-to-order (ATO): hybrid strategy, standard components, customized assembly

### Logistic Models And Operating Curves

- production operating curves: work in process, output rate, throughput time
- storage operating curves: stock level, service level, delivery delay

### Transportation Management

- process steps: requirements analysis, transport planning, departure handling, arrival handling
- revenue models: asset-based, non-asset/management, SaaS/subscription, 4PL integrator

### Digital Transformation (AI And IT)

- AI applications: route optimization, predictive maintenance, demand forecasting
- automation systems: e-way bill automation, consignment booking, real-time GPS tracking

### Indian Logistics Landscape

- infrastructure: national highways and NHDP, Indian Railways, major seaports
- challenges: complex tax laws such as GST/VAT, high logistics costs around 13% of GDP, infrastructural limitations
