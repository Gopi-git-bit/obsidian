# Revenue Model Strategy Source

This source was processed as a strategic decision artifact rather than a pricing note.

## Retained Signals

- Revenue-model choices should be made explicitly across asset ownership, service bundling, orchestration depth, and software monetization.
- The strongest logistics models increasingly behave like technology-enabled orchestration platforms rather than pure asset operators.
- Fintech, subscription, and performance-based pricing can become moats when they solve customer cash-flow or visibility pain.
- First-mile and cluster-based MSME operations are strong proving grounds for novel, asset-light business models.
- Strategic decisions under uncertainty should compare capital intensity, control, scalability, and segment fit rather than defaulting to one model.
