# Scenario Management Source

## Overview

This source adds a strategic adaptation layer around:

- parameterized business policies stored outside code
- scenario simulation for pricing, fleet, partnership, payment-term, and SLA change
- AI-assisted recommendation with human approval
- sandbox and gradual rollout for policy changes
- policy history and registry structures for auditable adaptability

## Core Takeaways

### 1. Scenario Management Is Really Policy Management

- The strongest idea is not just more scenario notes.
- The stronger pattern is storing operational policies in a controlled registry and simulating their impact before rollout.

### 2. Adaptation Should Be Controlled, Not Improvised

- Significant changes should require human approval.
- Testing in sandbox and rolling out gradually reduces operational shock.

### 3. Strategy Needs an Operational Feedback Loop

- AI can suggest adjustments, but policy change should remain observable and reviewable.
- Simulation quality becomes part of operational readiness.
