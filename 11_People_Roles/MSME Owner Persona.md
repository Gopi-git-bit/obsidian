# MSME Owner Persona

## Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Business Type | Manufacturing, trading, or services |
| Company Size | 5-50 employees |
| Shipment Frequency | 10-50/month |
| Decision Maker | Owner directly |
| Tech Proficiency | Medium - uses WhatsApp, basic apps |
| Budget Sensitivity | High |
| Location | Tier 2-3 cities common |

## Goals

- Minimize logistics cost as percentage of revenue
- Ensure reliable delivery for production schedules
- Reduce time spent on logistics management
- Protect product quality during transit
- Build reliable supply chain

## Pain Points

### Primary
- **Cost unpredictability** - Can't budget accurately
- **Communication gaps** - No updates on shipment
- **Documentation burden** - E-way bills, invoices
- **碎片化 providers** - Multiple transporters to manage

### Secondary
- Limited credit/working capital for logistics
- Finding vehicles for urgent needs
- Trust issues with unknown transporters
- Returns and reverse logistics

## Behavior Patterns

| Pattern | Insight |
|---------|---------|
| WhatsApp-first | Prefers WhatsApp over email/apps |
| Relationship-driven | Trust transporter over platform |
| Price-sensitive | Will switch for 5-10% savings |
| Risk-averse | Prefers known over unknown |
| Cash-flow conscious | Prefers deferred payment |
| Connectivity-variable | May operate in areas where app sessions or payments are interrupted |

## Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Platform takes too much commission" | Show time savings, reliability |
| "I know my transporter personally" | Offer relationship + tech benefits |
| "Prices are higher than my current" | Highlight hidden cost reduction |
| "My volumes are too small" | Prove unit economics work |

## Quote to Remember

*"I need to know where my material is without calling 10 times. If I can get that with a good price, I'll pay for it."*
