# Truck Aggregator Model

## Overview

A platform that connects shippers with truck owners/operators, providing technology to facilitate bookings without owning physical vehicles.

## Value Proposition

| For Shippers | For Truck Owners |
|--------------|------------------|
| Wide vehicle access | More load opportunities |
| Competitive pricing | Reduced deadhead |
| Technology + tracking | Business management tools |
| Single point of contact | Payment guarantee |

## Revenue Streams

### Primary
- **Commission** on each booking (10-20%)
- **Subscription fees** for frequent shippers
- **Premium services** (real-time tracking, insurance)

### Secondary
- **Data services** to fleet owners
- **Financing facilitation** (vehicle loans, fuel advances)
- **SaaS tools** for transporters

## Cost Structure

| Cost Category | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Technology | App development, maintenance, APIs |
| Operations | Customer support, dispute resolution |
| Marketing | Customer acquisition |
| Compliance | Documentation, insurance |
| Credit risk | Failed payments, disputes |

## Key Partners

- Fleet owners / Owner-operators
- Fuel stations
- Service stations
- Insurance providers
- Financial institutions

## Key Activities

1. **Demand generation** - Acquire shippers
2. **Supply aggregation** - Onboard trucks
3. **Matching** - [[Load Matching Algorithm]]
4. **Transaction facilitation** - Payment, documentation
5. **Dispute resolution** - POD, damage claims

## Key Resources

- Technology platform
- Mobile apps (shipper + driver)
- Customer service team
- Operations network

## Customer Segments

- [[MSME Shipper Pain Points]] - Primary
- Enterprise shippers - Premium segment
- Individual consignors - Opportunistic

## Channels

- Mobile app (primary)
- Web platform
- WhatsApp/Phone support
- API integration for large shippers

## Comparison with Traditional

| Aspect | Aggregator | Traditional |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Vehicle access | On-demand | Fixed fleet |
| Pricing | Dynamic | Negotiated |
| Tracking | Real-time | Manual |
| Payment | Platform-mediated | Direct |
| Scalability | High | Limited by fleet |

## Risks

- Driver reliability
- Price wars
- Regulatory challenges
- Driver retention
